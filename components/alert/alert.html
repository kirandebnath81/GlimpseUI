Alerts

Alerts are used to attract user's attraction for important information without interrupting the user's flow.

Standard Alerts

Create standard alert by using "alert" class.

Additionally use classes such as "alert-success" , "alert-error" , "alert-warn" , "alert-info" , for creating respective components.
